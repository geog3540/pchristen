Choropleth Mapping of Crude Death Rate in Illinois Counties

This project maps the crude death rates of all causes of death using death data from the CDC's WONDER Database for 2023. On this map, the darker colors represent counties with higher death rates, while the lighter colored areas represent counties with lower death rates.

Civilian Veterans Age 18+ in the Year 2000

This project is a proportional symbol map of the raw counts of the total number of civilian veterans in the counties of Illinois that are age 18 or older as recorded by the year 2000 Decennial Census. The size and of the circle symbols indicates the total number of veterans in that county.

Bivariate Mapping of the Percentage of Men with Bachelor's Degrees and the Percentage of the Veteran Population

This project compares the percentage of men with bachelor's degrees to the percentage of the veteran population based on the 2000 Decennial Census Data in the counties of Illinois. The color combinations represent the degree that each variable has high or low levels of both, as well as having low levels of one but high levels of the second. It is intended to see which counties have high percentages of veterans living there, as well as the percentage of men with at least one level of higher education.
